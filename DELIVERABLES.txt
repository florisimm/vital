===============================================================================
KEY DESIGN DECISIONS
===============================================================================

1. No ML/Models: Simple rule-based approach
2. Multiplier strategy: Bias stored as -0.1 to +0.1
3. Weekly analysis: Cron job prevents real-time overhead
4. Per-user adjustment: One adjustment record per user
5. Confidence-based: Don't trust adjustments with <10 data points
6. Immutable history: Never delete coach_overrides
7. RLS privacy: Users can't see others' patterns
